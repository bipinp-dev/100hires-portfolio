# Research Sources

The goal is to identify practitioners who actively implement AI-powered SEO for B2B SaaS.

Before selecting experts, research will be conducted across multiple platforms to reduce bias and improve coverage.

## Platforms to Research

- LinkedIn
- YouTube
- Personal websites and blogs
- Podcasts
- Conference speaker pages
- Company/team pages
- Newsletters
- Industry publications
- GitHub (where relevant)
- Search engines for independent discovery

## Evidence to Collect

For each candidate, collect evidence such as:

- Current role and company
- Recent content
- Practical case studies or experiments
- Public speaking or interviews
- Articles or blog posts
- Community recognition (citations, collaborations, guest appearances)

# Expert Selection Criteria

## Objective

Identify practitioners who actively implement AI-powered SEO for B2B SaaS companies and publicly share practical knowledge that can be used to build a future playbook.

## A candidate should ideally satisfy most of these criteria:

- Currently active in B2B SaaS
- Demonstrates practical SEO work
- Uses or discusses AI in SEO/content production
- Publishes educational content consistently
- Shares real experiments or case studies
- Has content available on LinkedIn, YouTube, podcasts, or a personal blog
- Has recognition from the industry through speaking, interviews, collaborations, or citations
